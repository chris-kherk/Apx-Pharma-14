Potential Risks and Side Effects

Steroids can help you bulk up fast, but they also have serious downsides. Here are the main things to watch out for:

  • Liver Damage: Steroids can harm your liver if you use them for a long time or in high doses. This could lead to problems like liver disease.
  • Heart Problems: Steroids might mess with your heart health, increasing your risk of heart attacks and strokes.
  • Hormonal Imbalances: Using steroids can throw off your body's natural hormone balance, causing all sorts of issues like infertility or irregular periods.
  • Acne: Many people who take steroids get acne, especially on their face, chest, and back.
  • Hair Loss: Steroids can make you lose hair, particularly on your head. This can be a big deal for some folks.
  • Gynecomastia: Sometimes, steroids can make men grow beast tissue, which can be embarrassing and uncomfortable.
  • Shrunken Testicles: Steroids can make your testicles shrink, which might affect your fertility and sex drive.
  • Mood Swings and Aggression: Some people notice changers in their mood when they're on steroids, like feeling angrier or more anxious than usual.

It's super important to be careful with steroids and only use them under the guidance of a doctor. They can be dangerous if you don't know what you're doing.

Limit drinking alcohol, smoking, and using recreational drugs to reduce the extra dangers that come with using steroids.
